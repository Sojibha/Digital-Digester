Bio Gas Detection

Bio gas detection is crucial for ensuring safety and sustainability in energy management. Methane, a primary component of bio gas, is highly flammable and poses significant risks to both human health and the environment. Detecting bio gas leaks promptly can prevent potential disasters, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and optimize the use of renewable energy sources.

Our innovative project leverages IoT-based technologies to monitor and detect bio gas levels in real-time. This approach not only enhances safety but also contributes to a cleaner and greener future.
