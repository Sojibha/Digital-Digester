About the Project

Our university rooftop hosts an innovative research project aimed at converting organic waste into renewable energy. With a custom-built 500L anaerobic digester, we utilize food waste from the canteen as a feedstock for biogas production.

Project Impact

This project demonstrates the potential of renewable energy solutions in reducing carbon emissions and managing waste effectively. By producing biogas, we reduce dependency on fossil fuels and contribute to a cleaner, greener future.
